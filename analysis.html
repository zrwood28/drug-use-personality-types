ETL

The dataset used in this project contains records for 1885 respondents. Each respondent completed three personality tests: NEO-FFI-R, BIS-11, and ImpSS and provided their level of education, age, gender, country of residence and ethnicity. In addition, participants completed a questionnaire where they provided the time of their last use for 18 legal and illegal drugs (alcohol, amphetamines, amyl nitrite, benzodiazepine, cannabis, chocolate, cocaine, caffeine, crack, ecstasy, heroin, ketamine, legal highs, LSD, methadone, mushrooms, nicotine and volatile substance abuse). The target column, used to train the machine learning models, was created by assigning a 1 to any participant that had used any illegal (amphetamines, amyl nitrite, benzodiazepine, cocaine, ecstasy, heroin, ketamine, LSD, methamphetamine, mushrooms, and volatile substance abuse) drug in the past year.
